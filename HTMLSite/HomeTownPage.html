Home Town Page



Hyderabad is a city in the southern part of India, located in the state of Telangana. It is the fourth most populous city in India, with a population of over 10 million people. Hyderabad has a rich history and culture, and is known for its famous landmarks, delicious cuisine, and IT industry. The city is famous for its biryani, a mouth-watering rice dish cooked with meat and spices, which is a must-try for anyone visiting Hyderabad. Hyderabad is also home to many historical monuments, such as the Golconda Fort and the Qutub Shahi Tombs.
